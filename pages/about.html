TechLoc is a proprietary platform developed exclusively to streamline the operational life of our fleet managers and dispatchers. We understand that in logistics, time is the most valuable asset. Our goal is simple: to instantly locate the right partner, in the right location, at the exact moment needed.

By centralizing our network of service providers into a single, map-based interface, we eliminate the noise of traditional searches and ensure every dispatch is handled by a trusted professional.

Geo-Intelligent Search

Forget generic lists. Our system visualizes availability on the map, allowing dispatchers to find the closest qualified technician to any asset in real-time.

Curated Database

Every entry in our system—from locksmiths to heavy tow operators—is vetted for M&T standards. We maintain up-to-date contact info and capability records.

Operational Efficiency

Designed to facilitate our daily workflow. Reduced downtime, faster dispatching, and transparent communication between HQ and the field.
